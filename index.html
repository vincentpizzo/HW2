Background Info

My name is Vincent Paul Pizzo, but I rarely go by that. Vinnie (without the y) is a name shared by many in my family. Upon meeting someone new, after I introduce myself, I almost ALWAYS hear either 1. "Like 'My Cousin Vinny'?" (if the person is older than me) or 2. "Like from 'Jersey Shore'?" (if the person is my age). I love the movie, but I hate the show. In a cruel twist of fate, I've actually met a few of the "Jersey Shore" people. Coincidentally, my family is from New Jersey, and we happened to bump into a few of them. I was not impressed.
