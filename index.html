Background Info

My name is Vincent Paul Pizzo, but I rarely go by that. Vinnie (without the y) is a name shared by many in my family. Upon meeting someone new, after I introduce myself, I almost ALWAYS hear either 1. "Like 'My Cousin Vinny'?" (if the person is older than me) or 2. "Like from 'Jersey Shore'?" (if the person is my age). I love the movie, but I hate the show. In a cruel twist of fate, I've actually met a few of the "Jersey Shore" people. Coincidentally, my family is from New Jersey, and we happened to bump into a few of them. I was not impressed.

I grew up in Oswego, Illinois, recently voted one of the most boring towns in Illinois ( Yikes ). I'm stuck being the middle child, having an older sister and a younger brother. As for jobs I've been a grocery store cart-monkey, worked in a sweltering textbook warehouse, and even had a summer of being a Hawkeye Guide on campus for Orientation. My most recent job was over this summer with STSG right here in Tippie. I had an internship there, and was able to learn about many of the basics of computers and the processes involved with setting up a college with technology.

My lifestyle is pretty laid back. I enjoy running, playing basketball, tennis, and ping pong. Reading is a big hobby of mine, as well as watching movies. Admittedly, I'm nervous to be ending my college career, but hopeful for the future.
